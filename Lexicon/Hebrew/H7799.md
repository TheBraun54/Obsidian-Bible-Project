# Lexicon Entry H7799

**Lemma**: שׁוּשַׁן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shûwshan

**Pronunciation**: shoo-shan'

**Description**:
or שׁוֹשָׁן; or שֹׁשָׁן; and (feminine) שׁוֹשַׁנָּה; from שׂוּשׂ; a lily (from its whiteness), as aflower of architectural ornament; also a (straight) trumpet (from the tubular shape); lily, Shoshannim.
