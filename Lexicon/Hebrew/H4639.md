# Lexicon Entry H4639

**Lemma**: מַעֲשֶׂה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: maʻăseh

**Pronunciation**: mah-as-eh'

**Description**:
from עָשָׂה; an action (good or bad); generally, a transaction; abstractly, activity; by implication, a product (specifically, a poem) or (generally) property; act, art, [phrase] bakemeat, business, deed, do(-ing), labor, thing made, ware of making, occupation, thing offered, operation, possession, [idiom] well, (handy-, needle-, net-) work(ing, -manship), wrought.
