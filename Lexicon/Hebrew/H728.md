# Lexicon Entry H728

**Lemma**: אֲרַוְנָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼĂravnâh

**Pronunciation**: ar-av-naw'

**Description**:
or (by transposition) אוֹרְנָה; or אַרְנִיָּהlemma אַנִיָּה missing resh, corrected to אַרְנִיָּה; all by orthographical variation for אׇרְנָן; Aravnah (or Arnijah or Ornah), a Jebusite; Araunah.
