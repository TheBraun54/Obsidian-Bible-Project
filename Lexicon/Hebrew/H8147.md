# Lexicon Entry H8147

**Lemma**: שְׁנַיִם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shᵉnayim

**Pronunciation**: shen-ah'-yim

**Description**:
dual of שֵׁנִי; feminine שְׁתַּיִם; two; also (as ordinal) twofold; both, couple, double, second, twain, [phrase] twelfth, [phrase] twelve, [phrase] twenty (sixscore) thousand, twice, two.
