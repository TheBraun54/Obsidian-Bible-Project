# Lexicon Entry H7802

**Lemma**: שׁוּשַׁן עֵדוּת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Shûwshan ʻÊdûwth

**Pronunciation**: shoo-shan' ay-dooth'

**Description**:
or (plural of former) שׁוֹשַׁנִּים עֵדוּתlemma שׁוֹשַׁנִּיס עֵדוּת samekh, corrected to שׁוֹשַׁנִּים עֵדוּת; from שׁוּשַׁן and עֵדוּת; lily (or trumpet) of assemblage; Shushan-Eduth or Shoshannim-Eduth, the title of a popular song; Shoshannim-Eduth, Shushan-eduth.
