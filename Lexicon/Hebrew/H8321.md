# Lexicon Entry H8321

**Lemma**: שֹׂרֵק

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sôrêq

**Pronunciation**: so-rake'

**Description**:
or שׂוֹרֵק; and (feminine) שֹׂרֵקָה; from שָׁרַק in the sense of redness (compare שָׂרֻק); a vine stock (properly, one yielding purple grapes, the richest variety); choice(-st, noble) wine. Compare שָׂרוּק.
