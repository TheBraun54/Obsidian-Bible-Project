# Lexicon Entry H7975

**Lemma**: שִׁלֹחַ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Shilôach

**Pronunciation**: shee-lo'-akh

**Description**:
lemma שִׁלֹּחַ extra dagesh, corrected to שִׁלֹחַ; or (in imitation of שֶׁלַח) שֶׁלַח; (Nehemiah 3:15), from שָׁלַח; rill; Shiloach, a fountain of Jerusalem; Shiloah, Siloah.
