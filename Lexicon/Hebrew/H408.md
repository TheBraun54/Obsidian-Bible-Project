# Lexicon Entry H408

**Lemma**: אַל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼal

**Pronunciation**: al

**Description**:
a negative particle (akin to לֹא); not (the qualified negation, used as a deprecative); once (Job 24:25) as a noun, nothing; nay, neither, [phrase] never, no, nor, not, nothing (worth), rather than.
