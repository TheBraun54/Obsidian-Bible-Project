# Lexicon Entry H4284

**Lemma**: מַחֲשָׁבָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: machăshâbâh

**Pronunciation**: makh-ash-aw-baw'

**Description**:
or מַחֲשֶׁבֶת; from חָשַׁב; a contrivance, i.e. (concretely) a texture, machine, or (abstractly) intention, plan (whether bad, a plot; or good, advice); cunning (work), curious work, device(-sed), imagination, invented, means, purpose, thought.
