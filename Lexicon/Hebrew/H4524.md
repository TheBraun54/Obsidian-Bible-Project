# Lexicon Entry H4524

**Lemma**: מֵסַב

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mêçab

**Pronunciation**: may-sab'

**Description**:
plural masculine מְסִבִּים; or feminine מְסִבּוֹת; from סָבַב; a divan (as enclosing the room); abstractly (adverbial) around; that compass about, (place) round about, at table.
