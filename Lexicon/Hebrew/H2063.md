# Lexicon Entry H2063

**Lemma**: זֹאת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: zôʼth

**Pronunciation**: zothe'

**Description**:
irregular feminine of זֶה; this (often used adverb); hereby (-in, -with), it, likewise, the one (other, same), she, so (much), such (deed), that, therefore, these, this (thing), thus.
