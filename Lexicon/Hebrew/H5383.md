# Lexicon Entry H5383

**Lemma**: נָשָׁה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: nâshâh

**Pronunciation**: naw-shaw'

**Description**:
a primitive root (rather identical with נָשָׁה, in the sense of נָשָׁא); to lend or (by reciprocity) borrow on security or interest; creditor, exact, extortioner, lend, usurer, lend on (taker on) usury.
