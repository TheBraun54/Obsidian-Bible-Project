# Lexicon Entry H68

**Lemma**: אֶבֶן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼeben

**Pronunciation**: eh'-ben

**Description**:
from the root of בָּנָה through the meaning to build; a stone; [phrase] carbuncle, [phrase] mason, [phrase] plummet, (chalk-, hail-, head-, sling-) stone(-ny), (divers) weight(-s).
