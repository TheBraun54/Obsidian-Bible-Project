# Lexicon Entry H2898

**Lemma**: טוּב

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ṭûwb

**Pronunciation**: toob

**Description**:
from טוֹב; good (as a noun), in the widest sense, especially goodness (superlative concretely, the best), beauty, gladness, welfare; fair, gladness, good(-ness, thing, -s), joy, go well with.
