# Lexicon Entry H62

**Lemma**: אָבֵל בֵּית־מֲעַכָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼÂbêl Bêyth-Măʻakâh

**Pronunciation**: aw-bale' bayth ma-akaw'

**Description**:
from אָבֵל and בַּיִת and מַעֲכָה; meadow of Beth-Maakah; Abel of Beth-maakah, a place in Palestine; Abel-beth-maachah, Abel of Beth-maachah,
