# Lexicon Entry H6252

**Lemma**: עַשְׁתָּרוֹת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻAshtârôwth

**Pronunciation**: ash-taw-roth'

**Description**:
or עַשְׁתָּרֹת; plural of עַשְׁתְּרָה; Ashtaroth, the name of a Sidonian deity, and of a place East of the Jordan; Asharoth, Astaroth. See also בֵּית עַשְׁתָּרוֹת, עַשְׁתֹּרֶת, עַשְׁתְּרֹת קַרְנַיִם.
