# Lexicon Entry H7665

**Lemma**: שָׁבַר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâbar

**Pronunciation**: shaw-bar'

**Description**:
a primitive root; to burst (literally or figuratively); break (down, off, in pieces, up), broken (-hearted), bring to the birth, crush, destroy, hurt, quench, [idiom] quite, tear, view (by mistake for שָׂבַר).
