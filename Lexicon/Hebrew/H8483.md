# Lexicon Entry H8483

**Lemma**: תַּחְתִּים חׇדְשִׁי

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Tachtîym Chodshîy

**Pronunciation**: takh-teem' khod-shee'

**Description**:
apparently from the plural masculine of תַּחְתִּי or תַּחַת and חֹדֶשׁ; lower (ones) monthly; Tachtim-Chodshi, a place in Palestine; Tahtim-hodshi.
