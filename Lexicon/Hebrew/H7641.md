# Lexicon Entry H7641

**Lemma**: שִׁבֹּל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shibbôl

**Pronunciation**: shib-bole

**Description**:
or (feminine) שִׁבֹּלֶת; from the same as שֹׁבֶל; a stream (as flowing); also an ear of grain (as growing out); by analogy, a branch; branch, channel, ear (of corn), (water-)flood, Shibboleth. Compare סִבֹּלֶת.
