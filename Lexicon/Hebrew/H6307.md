# Lexicon Entry H6307

**Lemma**: פַּדָּן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Paddân

**Pronunciation**: pad-dawn'

**Description**:
from an unused root meaning to extend; a plateau; or פַּדַּן אֲרָם; from the same and אֲרָם; the table-land of Aram; Paddan or Paddan-Aram, a region of Syria; Padan, Padan-aram.
