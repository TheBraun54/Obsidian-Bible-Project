# Lexicon Entry H7827

**Lemma**: שְׁחֵלֶת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shᵉchêleth

**Pronunciation**: shekh-ay'-leth

**Description**:
lemma שְׁחֶלֶת second vowel, corrected to שְׁחֵלֶת; apparently from the same as שַׁחַל through some obscure idea, perhaps that of peeling off by concussion of sound; a scale or shell, i.e. the aromatic mussel.; onycha.
