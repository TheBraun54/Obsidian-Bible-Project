# Lexicon Entry H4374

**Lemma**: מְכַסֶּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mᵉkaççeh

**Pronunciation**: mek-as-seh'

**Description**:
from כָּסָה; a covering, i.e. garment; specifically, a coverlet (for a bed), an awning (from the sun); also the omentum (as covering the intestines); clothing, to cover, that which covereth.
