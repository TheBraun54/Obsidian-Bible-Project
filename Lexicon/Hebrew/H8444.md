# Lexicon Entry H8444

**Lemma**: תּוֹצָאָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tôwtsâʼâh

**Pronunciation**: to-tsaw-aw'

**Description**:
or תֹּצָאָה; from יָצָא; (only in plural collective) exit, i.e. (geographical) boundary, or (figuratively) deliverance, (actively) source; border(-s), going(-s) forth (out), issues, outgoings.
