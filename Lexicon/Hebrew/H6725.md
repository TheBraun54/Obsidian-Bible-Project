# Lexicon Entry H6725

**Lemma**: צִיּוּן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tsîyûwn

**Pronunciation**: tsee-yoon'

**Description**:
lemma צִיוּן missing dagesh, corrected to צִיּוּן; from the same as צִיָּה in the sense of conspicuousness (compare נָצַח); a monumental or guiding pillar; sign, title, waymark.
