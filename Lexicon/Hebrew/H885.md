# Lexicon Entry H885

**Lemma**: בְּאֵרֹת בְּנֵי־יַעֲקַן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Bᵉʼêrôth Bᵉnêy-Yaʻăqan

**Pronunciation**: be-ay-roth' be-nay' yah-a-can'

**Description**:
xlit Bᵉêrôth corrected to Bᵉʼêrôth; from the feminine plural of בְּאֵר, and the plural contraction of בֵּן, and יַעֲקָן; wells of (the) sons of Jaakan; Beeroth-Bene-Jaakan, a place in the Desert; Beeroth of the children of Jaakan.
