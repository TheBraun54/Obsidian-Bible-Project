# Lexicon Entry H8033

**Lemma**: שָׁם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâm

**Pronunciation**: shawm

**Description**:
a primitive particle (rather from the relative pronoun, אֲשֶׁר); there (transferring to time) then; often thither, or thence; in it, [phrase] thence, there (-in, [phrase] of, [phrase] out), [phrase] thither, [phrase] whither.
