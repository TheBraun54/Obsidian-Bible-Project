# Lexicon Entry H1268

**Lemma**: בֵּרוֹתָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Bêrôwthâh

**Pronunciation**: bay-ro-thaw'

**Description**:
or בֵּרֹתַי; probably from בְּרוֹתlemma בֻּרוֹת first vowel, corrected to בְּרוֹת; cypress or cypresslike; Berothah or Berothai, a place north of Palestine; Berothah, Berothai.
