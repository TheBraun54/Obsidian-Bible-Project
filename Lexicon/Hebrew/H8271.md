# Lexicon Entry H8271

**Lemma**: שְׁרֵא

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shᵉrêʼ

**Pronunciation**: sher-ay'

**Description**:
(Aramaic) a root corresponding to that of שֵׁרוּת; to free, separate; figuratively, to unravel, commence; by implication (of unloading beasts) to reside; begin, dissolve, dwell, loose.
