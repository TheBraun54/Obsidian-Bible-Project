# Lexicon Entry H798

**Lemma**: אַשְׁדּוֹת הַפִּסְגָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼAshdôwth hap-Piçgâh

**Pronunciation**: ash-doth' hap-pisgaw'

**Description**:
from the plural of אֲשֵׁדָה and פִּסְגָּה with the article interposed; ravines of the Pisgah; Ashdoth-Pisgah, a place east of the Jordan; Ashdoth-pisgah.
