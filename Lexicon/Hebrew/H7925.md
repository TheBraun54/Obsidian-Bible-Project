# Lexicon Entry H7925

**Lemma**: שָׁכַם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâkam

**Pronunciation**: shaw-kam'

**Description**:
a primitive root; properly, to incline (the shoulder to a burden); but used only as denominative from שְׁכֶם; literally, to load up (on the back of man or beast), i.e. to start early in the morning; (arise, be up, get (oneself) up, rise up) early (betimes), morning.
