# Lexicon Entry H4916

**Lemma**: מִשְׁלוֹחַ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mishlôwach

**Pronunciation**: mish-lo'-akh

**Description**:
or מִשְׁלֹחַ; also מִשְׁלָח; from שָׁלַח; a sending out, i.e. (abstractly) presentation (favorable), or seizure (unfavorable); also (concretely) a place of dismissal, or a business to be discharged; to lay, to put, sending (forth), to set.
