# Lexicon Entry H6439

**Lemma**: פְּנוּאֵל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Pᵉnûwʼêl

**Pronunciation**: pen-oo-ale'

**Description**:
or (more properly,) פְּנִיאֵל; from פָּנָה and אֵל; face of God; Penuel or Peniel, a place East of Jordan; also (as Penuel) the name of two Israelites; Peniel, Penuel.
