# Lexicon Entry H8083

**Lemma**: שְׁמֹנֶה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shᵉmôneh

**Pronunciation**: shem-o-neh'

**Description**:
or שְׁמוֹנֶה; feminine שְׁמֹנָה; or שְׁמוֹנָה; apparently from שָׁמֵן through the idea of plumpness; a cardinal number, eight (as if a surplus above the 'perfect' seven); also (as ordinal) eighth; eight(-een, -eenth), eighth.
