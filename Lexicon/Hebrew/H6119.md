# Lexicon Entry H6119

**Lemma**: עָקֵב

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻâqêb

**Pronunciation**: aw-kabe'

**Description**:
or (feminine) עִקְּבָה; from עָקַב; a heel (as protuberant); hence, a track; figuratively, the rear (of an army); heel, (horse-) hoof, last, lier in wait (by mistake for עָקֵב), (foot-) step.
