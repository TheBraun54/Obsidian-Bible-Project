# Lexicon Entry H8454

**Lemma**: תּוּשִׁיָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tûwshîyâh

**Pronunciation**: too-shee-yaw'

**Description**:
or תֻּשִׁיָּה; from an unused root probably meaning to substantiate; support or (by implication) ability, i.e. (direct) help, (in purpose) an undertaking, (intellectual) understanding; enterprise, that which (thing as it) is, substance, (sound) wisdom, working.
