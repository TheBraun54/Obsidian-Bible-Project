# Lexicon Entry H8407

**Lemma**: תִּגְלַת פִּלְאֶסֶר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Tiglath Pilʼeçer

**Pronunciation**: tig-lath' pil-eh'-ser

**Description**:
or תִּגְלַת פְּלֶסֶר; or תִּלְגַּת פִּלְנְאֶסֶר or תִּלְגַּת פִּלְנֶסֶר; of foreign derivation; Tiglath-Pileser or Tilgath-pilneser, an Assyryrian king; Tiglath-pileser, Tilgath-pilneser.
