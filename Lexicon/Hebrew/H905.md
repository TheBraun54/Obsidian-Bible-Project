# Lexicon Entry H905

**Lemma**: בַּד

**Transliteration (xlit)**: bad

**Pronunciation**: bad

**Description**:
from בָּדַד; properly, separation; by implication, a part of the body, branch of atree, bar forcarrying; figuratively, chief of a city; especially (with prepositional prefix) as an adverb, apart, only, besides; alone, apart, bar, besides, branch, by self, of each alike, except, only, part, staff, strength.
