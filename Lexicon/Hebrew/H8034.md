# Lexicon Entry H8034

**Lemma**: שֵׁם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shêm

**Pronunciation**: shame

**Description**:
a primitive word (perhaps rather from through the idea of definite and conspicuous position; compare שָׁמַיִם); an appellation, as amark or memorial of individuality; by implication honor, authority, character; [phrase] base, (in-) fame(-ous), named(-d), renown, report.
