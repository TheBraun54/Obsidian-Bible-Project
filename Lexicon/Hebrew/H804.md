# Lexicon Entry H804

**Lemma**: אַשּׁוּר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼAshshûwr

**Pronunciation**: ash-shoor'

**Description**:
or אַשֻּׁר; apparently from אָשַׁר (in the sense of successful); Ashshur, the second son of Shem; also his descendants and the country occupied by them (i.e. Assyria), its region and its empire; Asshur, Assur, Assyria, Assyrians. See אָשֻׁר.
