# Lexicon Entry H63

**Lemma**: אָבֵל הַשִּׁטִּים

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼÂbêl hash-Shiṭṭîym

**Pronunciation**: aw-bale' hash-shit-teem'

**Description**:
from אָבֵל and the plural of שִׁטָּה, with the article inserted; meadow of the acacias; Abel hash-Shittim, a place in Palestine; Abelshittim.
