# Lexicon Entry H5854

**Lemma**: עַטְרוֹת בֵּית יוֹאָב

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻAṭrôwth bêyth Yôwʼâb

**Pronunciation**: at-roth' bayth yoawb'

**Description**:
from the same as עֲטָרוֹת and בַּיִת and יוֹאָב; crowns of the house of Joab; Atroth-beth-Joab, a place in Palestine; Ataroth the house of Joab.
