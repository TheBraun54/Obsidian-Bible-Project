# Lexicon Entry H8446

**Lemma**: תּוּר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tûwr

**Pronunciation**: toor

**Description**:
a primitive root; to meander (causatively, guide) about, especially fortrade or reconnoitring; chap(-man), sent to descry, be excellent, merchant(-man), search (out), seek, (e-) spy (out).
