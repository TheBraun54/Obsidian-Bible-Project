# Lexicon Entry H8267

**Lemma**: שֶׁקֶר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sheqer

**Pronunciation**: sheh'-ker

**Description**:
from שָׁקַר; an untruth; by implication, a sham (often adverbial); without a cause, deceit(-ful), false(-hood, -ly), feignedly, liar, [phrase] lie, lying, vain (thing), wrongfully.
