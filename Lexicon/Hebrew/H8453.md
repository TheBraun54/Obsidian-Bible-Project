# Lexicon Entry H8453

**Lemma**: תּוֹשָׁב

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tôwshâb

**Pronunciation**: to-shawb'

**Description**:
or תֹּשָׁב; (1 Kings 17:1), from יָשַׁב; a dweller (but not outlandish (נׇכְרִי)); especially (as distinguished from a native citizen (active participle of יָשַׁב) and a temporary inmate (גֵּר) or mere lodger (לוּן)); resident alien; foreigner, inhabitant, sojourner, stranger.
