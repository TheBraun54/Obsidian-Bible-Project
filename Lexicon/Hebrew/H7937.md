# Lexicon Entry H7937

**Lemma**: שָׁכַר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâkar

**Pronunciation**: shaw-kar'

**Description**:
a primitive root; to become tipsy; in a qualified sense, to satiate with astimulating drink or (figuratively) influence; (be filled with) drink (abundantly), (be, make) drunk(-en), be merry. (Superlative of שָׁקָה.)
