# Lexicon Entry H6890

**Lemma**: צֶרֶת הַשַּׁחַר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Tsereth hash-Shachar

**Pronunciation**: tseh'-reth hash-shakh'-ar

**Description**:
from the same as צֶרֶת and שַׁחַר with the article interposed; splendor of the dawn; Tsereth-hash-Shachar, a place in Palestine; Zareth-shahar.
