# Lexicon Entry H7434

**Lemma**: רָמַת הַמִּצְפֶּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Râmath ham-Mit-speh

**Pronunciation**: raw-math' ham-mitspeh'

**Description**:
from רָמָה and מִצְפֶּה with the article interpolated; height of the watch-tower; Ramath-ham-Mitspeh, a place in Palestine; Ramath-mizpeh.
