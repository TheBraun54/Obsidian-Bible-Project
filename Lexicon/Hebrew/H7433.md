# Lexicon Entry H7433

**Lemma**: רָמֹת גִּלעָד

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Râmôth Gilʻâd

**Pronunciation**: raw-moth' gil-awd'

**Description**:
or רָמוֹת גִּלעָד; (2 Chronicles 22:5), from the plural of רָמָה and גִּלְעָד; heights of Gilad; Ramoth-Gilad, a place East of the Jordan; Ramoth-gilead, Ramoth in Gilead. See also רָאמוֹת.
