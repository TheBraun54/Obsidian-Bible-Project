# Lexicon Entry H4751

**Lemma**: מַר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mar

**Pronunciation**: mar

**Description**:
or (feminine) מָרָה; from מָרַר; bitter (literally or figuratively); also (as noun) bitterness, or (adverbially) bitterly; [phrase] angry, bitter(-ly, -ness), chafed, discontented, [idiom] great, heavy.
