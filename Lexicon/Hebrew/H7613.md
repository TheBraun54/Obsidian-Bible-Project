# Lexicon Entry H7613

**Lemma**: שְׂאֵת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sᵉʼêth

**Pronunciation**: seh-ayth'

**Description**:
from נָשָׂא; an elevation or leprous scab; figuratively, elation or cheerfulness; exaltation in rank or character; be accepted, dignity, excellency, highness, raise up self, rising.
