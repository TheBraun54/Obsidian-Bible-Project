# Lexicon Entry H5397

**Lemma**: נְשָׁמָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: nᵉshâmâh

**Pronunciation**: nesh-aw-maw'

**Description**:
from נָשַׁם; a puff, i.e. wind, angry or vital breath, divine inspiration, intellect. or (concretely) an animal; blast, (that) breath(-eth), inspiration, soul, spirit.
