# Lexicon Entry H5178

**Lemma**: נְחֹשֶׁת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: nᵉchôsheth

**Pronunciation**: nekh-o'-sheth

**Description**:
for נְחוּשָׁה; copper, hence, something made of that metal, i.e. coin, a fetter; figuratively, base (as compared with gold or silver); brasen, brass, chain, copper, fetter (of brass), filthiness, steel.
