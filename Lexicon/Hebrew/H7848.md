# Lexicon Entry H7848

**Lemma**: שִׁטָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shiṭṭâh

**Pronunciation**: shit-taw'

**Description**:
feminine of a derivative (only in the plural שִׁטִּים; meaning the sticks of wood) from the same as שֹׁטֵט; the acacia (from its scourging thorns); shittah, shittim. See also בֵּית הַשִּׁטָּה.
