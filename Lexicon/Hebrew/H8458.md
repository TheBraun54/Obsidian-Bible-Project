# Lexicon Entry H8458

**Lemma**: תַּחְבֻּלָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tachbulâh

**Pronunciation**: takh-boo-law'

**Description**:
or תַּחְבּוּלָה; from חָבַל as denominative from חֶבֶל; (only in plural) properly, steerage (as a management of ropes), i.e. (figuratively) guidance or (by implication) a plan; good advice, (wise) counsels.
