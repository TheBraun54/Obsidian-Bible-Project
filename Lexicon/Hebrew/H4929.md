# Lexicon Entry H4929

**Lemma**: מִשְׁמָר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mishmâr

**Pronunciation**: mish-mawr'

**Description**:
from שָׁמַר; a guard (the man, the post or the prison); a deposit (figuratively); also (as observed) a usage (abstractly), or an example (concretely); diligence, guard, office, prison, ward, watch.
