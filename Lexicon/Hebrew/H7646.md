# Lexicon Entry H7646

**Lemma**: שָׂבַע

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sâbaʻ

**Pronunciation**: saw-bah'

**Description**:
or שָׂבֵעַ; a primitive root; to sate, i.e. fill to satisfaction (literally or figuratively); have enough, fill (full, self, with), be (to the) full (of), have plenty of, be satiate, satisfy (with), suffice, be weary of.
