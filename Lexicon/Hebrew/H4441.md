# Lexicon Entry H4441

**Lemma**: מַלְכִּיָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Malkîyâh

**Pronunciation**: mal-kee-yaw'

**Description**:
or מַלְכִּיָּהוּlemma מַלְכִּיָהוּ missing dagesh, corrected to מַלְכִּיָּהוּ; (Jeremiah [38:6), from מֶלֶךְ and יָהּ; king of (i.e. appointed by) Jah; Malkijah, the name of ten Israelites; Malchiah, Malchijah.
