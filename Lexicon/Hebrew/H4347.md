# Lexicon Entry H4347

**Lemma**: מַכָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: makkâh

**Pronunciation**: mak-kaw'

**Description**:
or (masculine) מַכֶּה; (plural only) from נָכָה; a blow (in 2 Chronicles 2:10, of the flail); by implication,; a wound; figuratively, carnage, also pestilence; beaten, blow, plague, slaughter, smote, [idiom] sore, stripe, stroke, wound(-ed).
