# Lexicon Entry H6621

**Lemma**: פֶּתַע

**Transliteration (xlit)**: pethaʻ

**Pronunciation**: peh'-thah

**Description**:
from an unused root meaning to open (the eyes); (compare פִּתְאוֹם); a wink, i.e. moment(used only (with or without preposition) adverbially, quickly or unexpectedly; at an instant, suddenly, [idiom] very.
