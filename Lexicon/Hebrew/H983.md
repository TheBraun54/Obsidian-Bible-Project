# Lexicon Entry H983

**Lemma**: בֶּטַח

**Transliteration (xlit)**: beṭach

**Pronunciation**: beh'takh

**Description**:
from בָּטַח; properly, a place of refuge; abstract, safety, both the fact (security) and the feeling (trust); often (adverb with or without preposition) safely; assurance, boldly, (without) care(-less), confidence, hope, safe(-ly, -ty), secure, surely.
