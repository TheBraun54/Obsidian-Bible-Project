# Lexicon Entry H8659

**Lemma**: תַּרְשִׁישׁ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Tarshîysh

**Pronunciation**: tar-sheesh'

**Description**:
probably the same as תַּרְשִׁישׁ (as the region of the stone, or the reverse); Tarshish, a place on the Mediterranean, hence, the ephithet of a merchant vessel (as if for or from that port); also the name of a Persian and of an Israelite; Tarshish, Tharshish.
