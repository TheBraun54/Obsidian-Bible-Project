# Lexicon Entry H4339

**Lemma**: מֵישָׁר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mêyshâr

**Pronunciation**: may-shawr'

**Description**:
from יָשַׁר; evenness, i.e. (figuratively) prosperity or concord; also straightness, i.e. (figuratively) rectitude (only in plural with singular sense; often adverbially); agreement, aright, that are equal, equity, (things that are) right(-eously, things), sweetly, upright(-ly, -ness).
