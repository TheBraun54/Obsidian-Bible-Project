# Lexicon Entry H2895

**Lemma**: טוֹב

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ṭôwb

**Pronunciation**: tobe

**Description**:
a primitive root,; to be (transitively, do or make) good (or well) in the widest sense; be (do) better, cheer, be (do, seem) good, (make) goodly, [idiom] please, (be, do, go, play) well.
