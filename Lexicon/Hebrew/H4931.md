# Lexicon Entry H4931

**Lemma**: מִשְׁמֶרֶת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mishmereth

**Pronunciation**: mish-mer'-reth

**Description**:
feminine of מִשְׁמָר; watch, i.e. the act (custody), or (concretely) the sentry, the post; objectively preservation, or (concretely) safe; figuratively observance, i.e. (abstractly) duty or (objectively) a usage or party; charge, keep, or to be kept, office, ordinace, safeguard, ward, watch.
