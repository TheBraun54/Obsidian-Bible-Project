# Lexicon Entry H8081

**Lemma**: שֶׁמֶן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shemen

**Pronunciation**: sheh'-men

**Description**:
from שָׁמַן; grease, especially liquid (as from the olive, often perfumed); figuratively, richness; anointing, [idiom] fat (things), [idiom] fruitful, oil(-ed), ointment, olive, [phrase] pine.
