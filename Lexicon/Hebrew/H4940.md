# Lexicon Entry H4940

**Lemma**: מִשְׁפָּחָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mishpâchâh

**Pronunciation**: mish-paw-khaw'

**Description**:
from שָׁפָה (compare שִׁפְחָה); a family, i.e. circle of relatives; figuratively, a class (of persons), a species (of animals) or sort (of things); by extension a tribe or people; family, kind(-red).
