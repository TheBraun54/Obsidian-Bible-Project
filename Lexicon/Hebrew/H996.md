# Lexicon Entry H996

**Lemma**: בֵּין

**Transliteration (xlit)**: bêyn

**Pronunciation**: bane

**Description**:
(sometimes in the plural masculine or feminine); properly, the constructive form of an otherwise unused noun from בִּין; a distinction; but used only as a preposition,; between (repeated before each noun, often with other particles); also as a conjunction, either...or; among, asunder, at, between (-twixt...and), [phrase] from (the widest), [idiom] in, out of, whether (it be...or), within.
