# Lexicon Entry H7620

**Lemma**: שָׁבוּעַ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâbûwaʻ

**Pronunciation**: shaw-boo'-ah

**Description**:
or שָׁבֻעַ; also (feminine) שְׁבֻעָה; properly, passive participle of שָׁבַע as a denominative of שֶׁבַע; literally, sevened, i.e. a week (specifically, of years); seven, week.
