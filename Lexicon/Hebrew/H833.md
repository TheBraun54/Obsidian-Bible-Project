# Lexicon Entry H833

**Lemma**: אָשַׁר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼâshar

**Pronunciation**: aw-shar'

**Description**:
or אָשֵׁר; a primitive root; to be straight (used in the widest sense, especially to be level, right, happy); figuratively, to go forward, be honest, proper; (call, be) bless(-ed, happy), go, guide, lead, relieve.
