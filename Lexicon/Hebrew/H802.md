# Lexicon Entry H802

**Lemma**: אִשָּׁה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼishshâh

**Pronunciation**: ish-shaw'

**Description**:
feminine of אִישׁ or אֱנוֹשׁ; irregular plural, נָשִׁים;(used in the same wide sense as אֱנוֹשׁ); a woman; (adulter) ess, each, every, female, [idiom] many, [phrase] none, one, [phrase] together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.
