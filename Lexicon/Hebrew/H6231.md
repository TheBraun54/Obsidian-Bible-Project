# Lexicon Entry H6231

**Lemma**: עָשַׁק

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻâshaq

**Pronunciation**: aw-shak'

**Description**:
a primitive root (compare עָשַׂק); to press upon, i.e. oppress, defraud, violate, overflow; get deceitfully, deceive, defraud, drink up, (use) oppress(-ion), -or), do violence (wrong).
