# Lexicon Entry H8354

**Lemma**: שָׁתָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâthâh

**Pronunciation**: shaw-thaw'

**Description**:
a primitive root; to imbibe (literally or figuratively); [idiom] assuredly, banquet, [idiom] certainly, drink(-er, -ing), drunk ([idiom] -ard), surely. (Prop. intensive of שָׁקָה.)
