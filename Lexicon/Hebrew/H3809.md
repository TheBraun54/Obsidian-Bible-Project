# Lexicon Entry H3809

**Lemma**: לָא

**Transliteration (xlit)**: lâʼ

**Pronunciation**: law

**Description**:
(Aramaic) or לָה; (Aramaic) (Daniel 4:32), corresponding to לֹא; {not (the simple or abs. negation); by implication, no;}; or even, neither, no(-ne, -r), (can-) not, as nothing, without.
