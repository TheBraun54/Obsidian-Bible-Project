# Lexicon Entry H7667

**Lemma**: שֶׁבֶר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sheber

**Pronunciation**: sheh'-ber

**Description**:
or שֵׁבֶר; from שָׁבַר; a fracture, figuratively, ruin; specifically, a solution (of a dream); affliction, breach, breaking, broken(-footed, -handed), bruise, crashing, destruction, hurt, interpretation, vexation.
