# Lexicon Entry H5019

**Lemma**: נְבוּכַדְנֶאצַּר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Nᵉbûwkadneʼtstsar

**Pronunciation**: neb-oo-kad-nets-tsar'

**Description**:
or נְבֻּכַדְנֶאצַּר; (2 Kings 24:1,10), or נְבוּכַדְנֶצַּר; (Esther 2:6; Daniel 1:18), or נְבוּכַדְרֶאצַּר; or נְבוּכַדְרֶאצּוֹר; (Ezra 2:1; Jeremiah 49:28), or foreign derivation; Nebukadnetstsar (or -retstsar, or -retstsor), king of Babylon; Nebuchadnezzar, Nebuchadrezzar.
