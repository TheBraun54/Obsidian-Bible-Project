# Lexicon Entry H8198

**Lemma**: שִׁפְחָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shiphchâh

**Pronunciation**: shif-khaw'

**Description**:
feminine from an unused root meaning to spread out (as a family; see מִשְׁפָּחָה); a female slave (as a member of the household); (bond-, hand-) maid(-en, -servant), wench, bondwoman, womanservant.
