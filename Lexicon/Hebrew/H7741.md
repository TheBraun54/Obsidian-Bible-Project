# Lexicon Entry H7741

**Lemma**: שָׁוֵה קִרְיָתַיִם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Shâvêh Qiryâthayim

**Pronunciation**: shaw-vay' kir-yawthah'-yim

**Description**:
from the same as שָׁוֵה and the dual of קִרְיָה; plain of a double city; Shaveh-Kirjathajim, a place East of the Jordan; Shaveh Kiriathaim.
