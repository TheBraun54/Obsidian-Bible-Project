# Lexicon Entry H7992

**Lemma**: שְׁלִישִׁי

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shᵉlîyshîy

**Pronunciation**: shel-ee-shee'

**Description**:
ordinal from שָׁלוֹשׁ; third; feminine athird (part); by extension, a third (day, year or time); specifically, a third-story cell); third (part, rank, time), three (years old).
