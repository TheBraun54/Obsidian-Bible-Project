# Lexicon Entry H6255

**Lemma**: עַשְׁתְּרֹת קַרְנַיִם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻAshtᵉrôth Qarnayim

**Pronunciation**: ash-ter-oth' kar-nah'-yim

**Description**:
from עַשְׁתָּרוֹת and the dual of קֶרֶן; Ashtaroth of (the) double horns (a symbol of the deity); Ashteroth-Karnaim, a place East of the Jordan; Ashtoreth Karnaim.
