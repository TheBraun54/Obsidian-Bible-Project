# Lexicon Entry H4405

**Lemma**: מִלָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: millâh

**Pronunciation**: mil-law'

**Description**:
from מָלַל (plural masculine as if from מִלֶּה; a word; collectively, a discourse; figuratively, a topic; [phrase] answer, by-word, matter, any thing (what) to say, to speak(-ing), speak, talking, word.
