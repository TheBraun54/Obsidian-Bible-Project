# Lexicon Entry H7639

**Lemma**: שְׂבָכָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sᵉbâkâh

**Pronunciation**: seb-aw-kaw'

**Description**:
feminine of שָׂבָךְ; a net-work, i.e. (in hunting) a snare, (in architecture) a ballustrade; also a reticulated ornament to apillar; checker, lattice, network, snare, wreath(-enwork).
