# Lexicon Entry H53

**Lemma**: אֲבִישָׁלוֹם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼĂbîyshâlôwm

**Pronunciation**: ab-ee-shaw-lome'

**Description**:
or (shortened) אַבְשָׁלוֹם ; from אָב and שָׁלוֹם; father of peace (i.e. friendly); Abshalom, a son of David; also (the fuller form) a later Israelite; Abishalom, Absalom.
