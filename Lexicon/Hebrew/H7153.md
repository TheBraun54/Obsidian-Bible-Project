# Lexicon Entry H7153

**Lemma**: קִרְיַת אַרְבַּע

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Qiryath ʼArbaʻ

**Pronunciation**: keer-yath' ar-bah'

**Description**:
or (with the article interposed) קִרְיַת הָאַרְבַּע; (Nehemiah 11:25), from קִרְיָה and אַרְבַּע or אַרְבַּע; city of Arba, or city of the four (giants); Kirjath-Arba or Kirjath-ha-Arba, a place in Palestine; Kirjath-arba.
