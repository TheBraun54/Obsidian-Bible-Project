# Lexicon Entry H6240

**Lemma**: עָשָׂר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻâsâr

**Pronunciation**: aw-sawr'

**Description**:
for עֶשֶׂר; ten (only in combination), i.e. -teen; also (ordinal) -teenth; (eigh-, fif-, four-, nine-, seven-, six-, thir-) teen(-th), [phrase] eleven(-th), [phrase] sixscore thousand, [phrase] twelve(-th).
