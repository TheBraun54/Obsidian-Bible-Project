# Lexicon Entry H5401

**Lemma**: נָשַׁק

**Transliteration (xlit)**: nâshaq

**Pronunciation**: naw-shak'

**Description**:
a primitive root (identical with נָשַׂק, through the idea of fastening up; compare חָזַק, חָשַׁק); to kiss, literally or figuratively (touch); also (as a mode of attachment), to equip with weapons; armed (men), rule, kiss, that touched.
