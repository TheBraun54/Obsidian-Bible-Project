# Lexicon Entry H6311

**Lemma**: פֹּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: pôh

**Pronunciation**: po

**Description**:
or פֹּא; (Job 38:11), or פּוֹ; probably from a primitive inseparable particle 'p' (of demonstrative force) and הוּא; this place (French ici), i.e. here or hence; here, hither, the one (other, this, that) side.
