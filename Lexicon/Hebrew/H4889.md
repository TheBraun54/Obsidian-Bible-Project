# Lexicon Entry H4889

**Lemma**: מַשְׁחִית

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mashchîyth

**Pronunciation**: mash-kheeth'

**Description**:
from שָׁחַת; destructive, i.e. (as noun) destruction, literally (specifically a snare) or figuratively (corruption); corruption, (to) destroy(-ing), destruction, trap, [idiom] utterly.
