# Lexicon Entry H4853

**Lemma**: מַשָּׂא

**Transliteration (xlit)**: massâʼ

**Pronunciation**: mas-saw'

**Description**:
from נָשָׂא; a burden; specifically, tribute, or (abstractly) porterage; figuratively, an utterance, chiefly adoom, especially singing; mental, desire; burden, carry away, prophecy, [idiom] they set, song, tribute.
