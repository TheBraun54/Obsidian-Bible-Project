# Lexicon Entry H8471

**Lemma**: תַּחְפַּנְחֵס

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Tachpanchêç

**Pronunciation**: takh-pan-khace'

**Description**:
or תְּחַפְנְחֵס; (Ezekiel 30:18), or תַּחְפְּנֵס; (Jeremiah 2:16), of Egyptian derivation; Tachpanches, Techaphneches or Tachpenes, a place in Egypt; Tahapanes, Tahpanhes, Tehaphnehes.
