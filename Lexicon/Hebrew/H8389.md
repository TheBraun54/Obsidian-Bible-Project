# Lexicon Entry H8389

**Lemma**: תֹּאַר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tôʼar

**Pronunciation**: to'-ar

**Description**:
from תָּאַר; outline, i.e. figure or appearance; [phrase] beautiful, [idiom] comely, countenance, [phrase] fair, [idiom] favoured, form, [idiom] goodly, [idiom] resemble, visage.
