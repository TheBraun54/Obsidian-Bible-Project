# Lexicon Entry H441

**Lemma**: אַלּוּף

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼallûwph

**Pronunciation**: al-loof'

**Description**:
or (shortened) אַלֻּף; from אָלַף; familiar; a friend, also gentle; hence, a bullock (as being tame; applied, although masculine, to a cow); and so, a chieftain (as notable, like neat cattle); captain, duke, (chief) friend, governor, guide, ox.
