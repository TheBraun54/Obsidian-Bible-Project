# Lexicon Entry H6446

**Lemma**: פַּס

**Transliteration (xlit)**: paç

**Pronunciation**: pas

**Description**:
from פָּסַס; properly, the palm (of the hand) or sole (of the foot) (compare פַּס); by implication (plural); a long and sleeved tunic (perhaps simply a wide one; from the original sense of the root, i.e. of many breadths); (divers) colours.
