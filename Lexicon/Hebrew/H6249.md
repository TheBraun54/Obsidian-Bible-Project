# Lexicon Entry H6249

**Lemma**: עַשְׁתֵּי

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻashtêy

**Pronunciation**: ash-tay'

**Description**:
apparently masculine plural construction of עֶשֶׁת in the sense of an afterthought (used only in connection with עָשָׂר in lieu of אֶחָד); eleven or (ordinal) eleventh; [phrase] eleven(-th).
