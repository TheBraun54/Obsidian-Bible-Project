# Lexicon Entry H783

**Lemma**: אַרְתַּחְשַׁשְׁתָּא

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼArtachshashtâʼ

**Pronunciation**: ar-takh-shash-taw'

**Description**:
or אַרְתַּחְשַׁשְׁתְּא; or by permutation אַרְתַּחְשַׁסְתְּא; of foreign origin; Artachshasta (or Artaxerxes), a title (rather than name) of several Persian kings; Artaxerxes.
