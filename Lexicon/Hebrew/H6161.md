# Lexicon Entry H6161

**Lemma**: עֲרֻבָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻărubbâh

**Pronunciation**: ar-oob-baw'

**Description**:
feminine passive participle of עֲנַמֶּלֶךְ in the sense of a bargain or exchange; something given as security, i.e. (literally) a token (of safety) or (metaphorically) a bondsman; pledge, surety.
