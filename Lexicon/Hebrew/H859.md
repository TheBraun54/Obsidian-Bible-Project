# Lexicon Entry H859

**Lemma**: אַתָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼattâh

**Pronunciation**: at-taw'

**Description**:
or (shortened); אַתָּ; or אַת; feminine (irregular) sometimes אַתִּי; plural masculine אַתֶּם; feminine אַתֶּן; or אַתֵּנָהlemma אתֵּנָה missing vowel, corrected to אַתֵּנָה; or אַתֵּנָּה; a primitive pronoun of the second person; thou and thee, or (plural) ye and you; thee, thou, ye, you.
