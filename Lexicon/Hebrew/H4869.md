# Lexicon Entry H4869

**Lemma**: מִשְׂגָּב

**Transliteration (xlit)**: misgâb

**Pronunciation**: mis-gawb'

**Description**:
from שָׂגַב; properly, a cliff (or other lofty or inaccessible place); abstractly, altitude; figuratively, a refuge; defence, high fort (tower), refuge, Misgab, a place in Moab; Misgab.
