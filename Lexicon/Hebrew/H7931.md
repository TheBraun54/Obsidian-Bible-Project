# Lexicon Entry H7931

**Lemma**: שָׁכַן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâkan

**Pronunciation**: shaw-kan'

**Description**:
a primitive root (apparently akin (by transmission) to שָׁכַב through the idea of lodging; compare סִכְלוּת, שָׁכַם); to reside or permanently stay (literally or figuratively); abide, continue, (cause to, make to) dwell(-er), have habitation, inhabit, lay, place, (cause to) remain, rest, set (up).
