# Lexicon Entry H7565

**Lemma**: רֶשֶׁף

**Transliteration (xlit)**: resheph

**Pronunciation**: reh'-shef

**Description**:
from שָׂרַף; a live coal; by analogy lightning; figuratively, an arrow, (as flashing through the air); specifically, fever; arrow, (burning) coal, burning heat, [phrase] spark, hot thunderbolt.
