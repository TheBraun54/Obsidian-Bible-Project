# Lexicon Entry H8065

**Lemma**: שָׁמַיִן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâmayin

**Pronunciation**: shaw-mah'-yin

**Description**:
(Aramaic) corresponding to שָׁמַיִם; {the sky (as aloft; the dual perhaps alluding to the visible arch in which the clouds move, as well as to the higher ether where the celestial bodies revolve)}; heaven.
