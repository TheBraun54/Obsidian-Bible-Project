# Lexicon Entry H8641

**Lemma**: תְּרוּמָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tᵉrûwmâh

**Pronunciation**: ter-oo-maw'

**Description**:
or תְּרֻמָה; (Deuteronomy 12:11), from רוּם; a present (as offered up), especially in sacrifice or as tribute; gift, heave offering (shoulder), oblation, offered(-ing).
