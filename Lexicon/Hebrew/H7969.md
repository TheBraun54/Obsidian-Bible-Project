# Lexicon Entry H7969

**Lemma**: שָׁלוֹשׁ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâlôwsh

**Pronunciation**: shaw-loshe'

**Description**:
or שָׁלֹשׁ; masculine שְׁלוֹשָׁה; or שְׁלֹשָׁה; a primitive number; three; occasionally (ordinal) third, or (multiple) thrice; [phrase] fork, [phrase] often(-times), third, thir(-teen, -teenth), three, [phrase] thrice. Compare שָׁלִישׁ.
