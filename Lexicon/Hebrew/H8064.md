# Lexicon Entry H8064

**Lemma**: שָׁמַיִם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâmayim

**Pronunciation**: shaw-mah'-yim

**Description**:
dual of an unused singular שָׁמֶה; from an unused root meaning to be lofty; the sky (as aloft; the dual perhaps alluding to the visible arch in which the clouds move, as well as to the higher ether where the celestial bodies revolve); air, [idiom] astrologer, heaven(-s).
