# Lexicon Entry H8441

**Lemma**: תּוֹעֵבַה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tôwʻêbah

**Pronunciation**: to-ay-baw'

**Description**:
or תֹּעֵבַה; feminine active participle of תַּעָב; properly, something disgusting (morally), i.e. (as noun) an abhorrence; especially idolatry or (concretely) an idol; abominable (custom, thing), abomination.
