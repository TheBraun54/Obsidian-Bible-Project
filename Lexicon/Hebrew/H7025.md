# Lexicon Entry H7025

**Lemma**: קִיר חֶרֶשׂ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Qîyr Cheres

**Pronunciation**: keer kheh'-res

**Description**:
or (feminine of the latter word) קִיר חֲרֶשֶׂת; from קִיר and חֶרֶשׂ; fortress of earthenware; Kir-Cheres or Kir-Chareseth, a place in Moab; Kir-haraseth, Kir-hareseth, Kirharesh, Kir-heres.
