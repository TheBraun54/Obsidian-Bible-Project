# Lexicon Entry H4294

**Lemma**: מַטֶּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: maṭṭeh

**Pronunciation**: mat-teh'

**Description**:
or (feminine) מַטָּה; from נָטָה; a branch (as extending); figuratively, a tribe; also a rod, whether for chastising (figuratively, correction), ruling (a sceptre), throwing (a lance), or walking (a staff; figuratively, a support of life, e.g. bread); rod, staff, tribe.
