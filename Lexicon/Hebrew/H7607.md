# Lexicon Entry H7607

**Lemma**: שְׁאֵר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shᵉʼêr

**Pronunciation**: sheh-ayr'

**Description**:
from שָׁאַר; flesh (as swelling out), as living or forfood; generally food of any kind; figuratively, kindred by blood; body, flesh, food, (near) kin(-sman, -swoman), near (nigh) (of kin).
