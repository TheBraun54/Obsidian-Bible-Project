# Lexicon Entry H951

**Lemma**: בּוֹקֵר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: bôwqêr

**Pronunciation**: bo-kare'

**Description**:
lemma בּוֹקִר second vowel, corrected to בּוֹקֵר; xlit bôwkêr corrected to bôwqêr; properly, active participle from בָּקַר as denominative from בָּקָר; a cattle-tender; herdman.
