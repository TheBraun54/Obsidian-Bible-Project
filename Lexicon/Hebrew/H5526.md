# Lexicon Entry H5526

**Lemma**: סָכַךְ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: çâkak

**Pronunciation**: saw-kak'

**Description**:
or שָׂכַךְ; (Exodus 33:22), a primitive root; properly, to entwine as ascreen; by implication, to fence in, cover over, (figuratively) protect; cover, defence, defend, hedge in, join together, set, shut up.
