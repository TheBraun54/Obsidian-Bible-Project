# Lexicon Entry H8598

**Lemma**: תַּפּוּחַ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tappûwach

**Pronunciation**: tap-poo'-akh

**Description**:
from נָפַח; an apple (from its fragrance), i.e. the fruit or the tree (probably includ. others of the pome order, as the quince, the orange, etc.); apple (tree). See also בֵּית תַּפּוּחַ.
