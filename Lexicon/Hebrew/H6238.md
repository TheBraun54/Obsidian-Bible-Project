# Lexicon Entry H6238

**Lemma**: עָשַׁר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻâshar

**Pronunciation**: aw-shar'

**Description**:
a primitive root; properly, to accumulate; chiefly (specifically) to grow (causatively, make) rich; be(-come, en-, make, make self, wax) rich, make (1 Kings 22:48 marg). See עָשָׂר.
