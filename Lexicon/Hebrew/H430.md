# Lexicon Entry H430

**Lemma**: אֱלֹהִים

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼĕlôhîym

**Pronunciation**: el-o-heem'

**Description**:
plural of אֱלוֹהַּ; gods in the ordinary sense; but specifically used (in the plural thus, especially with the article) of the supreme God; occasionally applied by way of deference to magistrates; and sometimes as a superlative; angels, [idiom] exceeding, God (gods) (-dess, -ly), [idiom] (very) great, judges, [idiom] mighty.
