# Lexicon Entry H4932

**Lemma**: מִשְׁנֶה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mishneh

**Pronunciation**: mish-neh'

**Description**:
from שָׁנָה; properly, a repetition, i.e. a duplicate (copy of a document), or a double (in amount); by implication, a second (in order, rank, age, quality or location); college, copy, double, fatlings, next, second (order), twice as much.
