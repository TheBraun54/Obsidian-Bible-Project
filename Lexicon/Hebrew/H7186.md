# Lexicon Entry H7186

**Lemma**: קָשֶׁה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: qâsheh

**Pronunciation**: kaw-sheh'

**Description**:
from קָשָׁה; severe (in various applications); churlish, cruel, grievous, hard((-hearted), thing), heavy, [phrase] impudent, obstinate, prevailed, rough(-ly), sore, sorrowful, stiff(necked), stubborn, [phrase] in trouble.
