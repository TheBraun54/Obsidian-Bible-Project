# Lexicon Entry H7936

**Lemma**: שָׂכַר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sâkar

**Pronunciation**: saw-kar'

**Description**:
or (by permutation) סָכַר; (Ezra 4:5), a primitive root (apparently akin (by prosthesis) to כָּרָה through the idea of temporary purchase; compare שָׁכַר); to hire; earn wages, hire (out self), reward, [idiom] surely.
