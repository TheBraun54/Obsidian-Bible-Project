# Lexicon Entry H6286

**Lemma**: פָּאַר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: pâʼar

**Pronunciation**: paw-ar'

**Description**:
a primitive root; also denominative from פְּאֹרָה,; to gleam, i.e. (causatively) embellish; figuratively, to boast; also to explain (i.e. make clear) oneself; to shake a tree; beautify, boast self, go over the boughs, glorify (self), glory, vaunt self.
