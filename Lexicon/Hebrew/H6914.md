# Lexicon Entry H6914

**Lemma**: קִבְרוֹת הַתַּאֲוָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Qibrôwth hat-Taʼă-vâh

**Pronunciation**: kib-roth' hat-tahav-aw'

**Description**:
from the feminine plural of קֶבֶר and תַּאֲוָה with the article interposed; graves of the longing; Kibroth-hat-Taavh, a place in the Desert; Kibroth-hattaavah.
