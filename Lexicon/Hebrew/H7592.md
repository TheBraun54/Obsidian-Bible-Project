# Lexicon Entry H7592

**Lemma**: שָׁאַל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâʼal

**Pronunciation**: shaw-al'

**Description**:
or שָׁאֵל; a primitive root; to inquire; by implication, to request; by extension, to demand; ask (counsel, on), beg, borrow, lay to charge, consult, demand, desire, [idiom] earnestly, enquire, [phrase] greet, obtain leave, lend, pray, request, require, [phrase] salute, [idiom] straitly, [idiom] surely, wish.
