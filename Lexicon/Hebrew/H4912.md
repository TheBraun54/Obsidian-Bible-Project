# Lexicon Entry H4912

**Lemma**: מָשָׁל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mâshâl

**Pronunciation**: maw-shawl'

**Description**:
apparently from מָשַׁל in some original sense of superiority in mental action; properly, a pithy maxim, usually of metaphorical nature; hence, a simile (as an adage, poem, discourse); byword, like, parable, proverb.
