# Lexicon Entry H4945

**Lemma**: מַשְׁקֶה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mashqeh

**Pronunciation**: mash-keh'

**Description**:
from שָׁקָה; properly, causing to drink, i.e. a butler; by implication (intransitively), drink (itself); figuratively, a well-watered region; butler(-ship), cupbearer, drink(-ing), fat pasture, watered.
