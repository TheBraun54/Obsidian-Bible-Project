# Lexicon Entry H8562

**Lemma**: תַּמְרוּק

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tamrûwq

**Pronunciation**: tam-rook'

**Description**:
or תַּמְרֻק; or תַּמְרִיק; from מָרַק; properly, a scouring, i.e. soap or perfumery forthe bath; figuratively, a detergent; [idiom] cleanse, (thing for) purification(-fying).
