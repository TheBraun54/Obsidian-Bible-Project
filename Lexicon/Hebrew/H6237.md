# Lexicon Entry H6237

**Lemma**: עָשַׂר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻâsar

**Pronunciation**: aw-sar'

**Description**:
a primitive root (identical with עָשַׁר); to accumulate; but used only as denominative from עֶשֶׂר; to tithe, i.e. to take or give atenth; [idiom] surely, give (take) the tenth, (have, take) tithe(-ing, -s), [idiom] truly.
