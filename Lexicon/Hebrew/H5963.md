# Lexicon Entry H5963

**Lemma**: עַלְמֹן דִּבְלָתָיְמָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻAlmôn Diblâthâyᵉmâh

**Pronunciation**: al-mone' dib-lawthaw'-yem-aw

**Description**:
from the same as עַלְמוֹן and the dual of הֻיְּדָה (compare בֵּית דִּבְלָתַיִם) with enclitic of direction; Almon towards Diblathajim; Almon-Diblathajemah, a place in Moab; Almondilathaim.
