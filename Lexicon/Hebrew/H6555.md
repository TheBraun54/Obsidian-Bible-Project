# Lexicon Entry H6555

**Lemma**: פָּרַץ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: pârats

**Pronunciation**: paw-rats'

**Description**:
lemma פּרַץ missing vowel, corrected to פָּרַץ; a primitive root; to break out (in many applications, direct and indirect, literal and figurative); [idiom] abroad, (make a) breach, break (away, down, -er, forth, in, up), burst out, come (spread) abroad, compel, disperse, grow, increase, open, press, scatter, urge.
