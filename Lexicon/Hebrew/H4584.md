# Lexicon Entry H4584

**Lemma**: מָעוֹן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Mâʻôwn

**Pronunciation**: maw-ohn'

**Description**:
the same as מָעוֹן; a residence; Maon, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine; Maon, Maonites. Compare בֵּית בַּעַל מְעוֹן, מְעוּנִיlemma מְעוּנָי third vowel, corrected to מְעוּנִי.
