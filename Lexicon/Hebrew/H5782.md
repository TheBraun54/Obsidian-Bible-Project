# Lexicon Entry H5782

**Lemma**: עוּר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻûwr

**Pronunciation**: oor

**Description**:
a primitive root (rather identical with through the idea of opening the eyes); to wake (literally or figuratively); (a-) wake(-n, up), lift up (self), [idiom] master, raise (up), stir up (self).
