# Lexicon Entry H4924

**Lemma**: מַשְׁמָן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mashmân

**Pronunciation**: mash-mawn'

**Description**:
from שָׁמַן; fat, i.e. (literally and abstractly) fatness; but usually (figuratively and concretely) a rich dish, a fertile field, a robust man; fat (one, -ness, -test, -test place).
