# Lexicon Entry H8549

**Lemma**: תָּמִים

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tâmîym

**Pronunciation**: taw-meem'

**Description**:
from תָּמַם; entire (literally, figuratively or morally); also (as noun) integrity, truth; without blemish, complete, full, perfect, sincerely (-ity), sound, without spot, undefiled, upright(-ly), whole.
