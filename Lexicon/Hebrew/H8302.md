# Lexicon Entry H8302

**Lemma**: שִׁרְיוֹן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shiryôwn

**Pronunciation**: shir-yone'

**Description**:
or שִׁרְיֹן; and שִׁרְיָן; also (feminine) שִׁרְיָה; and שִׁרְיֹנָה; from שָׁרָה in the original sense of turning; a corslet (as if twisted); breastplate, coat of mail, habergeon, harness. See סִרְיֹן.
