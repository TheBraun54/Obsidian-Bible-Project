# Lexicon Entry H8184

**Lemma**: שְׂעֹרָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sᵉʻôrâh

**Pronunciation**: seh-o-raw'

**Description**:
or שְׂעוֹרָה (feminine meaning the plant); and (masculine meaning the grain); also שְׂעֹר; or שְׂעוֹר; from שָׂעַר in the sense of roughness; barley (as villose); barley.
