# Lexicon Entry H8394

**Lemma**: תָּבוּן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tâbûwn

**Pronunciation**: taw-boon'

**Description**:
and (feminine) תְּבוּנָה; or תּוֹבֻנָה; from בִּין; intelligence; by implication, an argument; by extension, caprice; discretion, reason, skilfulness, understanding, wisdom.
