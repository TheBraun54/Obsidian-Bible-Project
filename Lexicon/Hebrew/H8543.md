# Lexicon Entry H8543

**Lemma**: תְּמוֹל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tᵉmôwl

**Pronunciation**: tem-ole'

**Description**:
or תְּמֹל; probably for אֶתְמוֹל; sometimes with  שִׁלְשׁוֹם; properly, ago, i.e. a (short or long) time since; especially yesterday, or  day before yesterday; [phrase] before (-time), [phrase] these (three) days, [phrase] heretofore, [phrase] time past, yesterday.
