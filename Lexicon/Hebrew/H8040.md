# Lexicon Entry H8040

**Lemma**: שְׂמֹאול

**Transliteration (xlit)**: sᵉmôʼwl

**Pronunciation**: sem-ole'

**Description**:
or שְׂמֹאל; a primitive word (rather perhaps from the same as שִׂמְלָה (by insertion of the aleph) through the idea of wrapping up); properly, dark (as enveloped), i.e. the north; hence (by orientation), the left hand; left (hand, side).
