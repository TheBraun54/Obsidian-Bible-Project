# Lexicon Entry H7198

**Lemma**: קֶשֶׁת

**Transliteration (xlit)**: qesheth

**Pronunciation**: keh'-sheth

**Description**:
from קָשָׁה in the original sense (of קוֹשׁ) of bending; a bow, forshooting (hence, figuratively, strength) or the iris; [idiom] arch(-er), [phrase] arrow, bow(-man, -shot).
