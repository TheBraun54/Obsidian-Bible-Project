# Lexicon Entry H8429

**Lemma**: תְּוַהּ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tᵉvahh

**Pronunciation**: tev-ah'

**Description**:
(Aramaic) corresponding to תָּמַהּ or perhaps to שָׁאָה through the idea of sweeping to ruin (compare תָּוָה); to amaze, i.e. (reflex. by implication) take alarm; be astonied.
