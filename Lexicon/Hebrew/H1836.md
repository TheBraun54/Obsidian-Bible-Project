# Lexicon Entry H1836

**Lemma**: דֵּן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: dên

**Pronunciation**: dane

**Description**:
(Aramaic) an orthographical variation of דֵּךְ; this; (afore-) time, [phrase] after this manner, here (-after), one...another, such, there(-fore), these, this (matter), [phrase] thus, where(-fore), which.
