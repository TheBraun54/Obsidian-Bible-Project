# Lexicon Entry H521

**Lemma**: אַמָּה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʼammâh

**Pronunciation**: am-maw'

**Description**:
(Aramaic) corresponding to אַמָּה; {properly, a mother (i.e. unit of measure, or the fore-arm (below the elbow), i.e. a cubit; also a door-base (as a bond of the entrance)}; cubit.
