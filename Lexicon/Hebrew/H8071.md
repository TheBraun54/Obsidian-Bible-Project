# Lexicon Entry H8071

**Lemma**: שִׂמְלָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: simlâh

**Pronunciation**: sim-law'

**Description**:
perhaps by permutation for the feminine of סֶמֶל (through the idea of a cover assuming the shape of the object beneath); a dress, especially a mantle; apparel, cloth(-es, -ing), garment, raiment. Compare שַׂלְמָה.
