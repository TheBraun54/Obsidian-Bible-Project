# Lexicon Entry H7417

**Lemma**: רִמּוֹן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: Rimmôwn

**Pronunciation**: rim-mone'

**Description**:
or (shorter) (רִמֹּן; or רִמּוֹנוֹ; (1 Chronicles 6:62 (אֶבֶץ)), the same as רִמּוֹן; in Joshua 19:13: הַמְּתֹאָר is mistaken for part of a name; article with the passive participle of תָּאַר; the (one) marked off, i.e. which pertains).; Rimmon, the name of a Syrian deity, also of five places in Palestine; Remmon, Rimmon.
