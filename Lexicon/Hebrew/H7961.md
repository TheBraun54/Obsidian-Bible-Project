# Lexicon Entry H7961

**Lemma**: שָׁלֵו

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shâlêv

**Pronunciation**: shaw-lave'

**Description**:
or שָׁלֵיו; feminine שְׁלֵוָה; from שָׁלָה; tranquil; (in a bad sense) careless; abstractly, security; (being) at ease, peaceable, (in) prosper(-ity), quiet(-ness), wealthy.
