# Lexicon Entry H7223

**Lemma**: רִאשׁוֹן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: riʼshôwn

**Pronunciation**: ree-shone'

**Description**:
or רִאשֹׁן; from רִאשָׁה; first, in place, time or rank (as adjective or noun); ancestor, (that were) before(-time), beginning, eldest, first, fore(-father) (-most), former (thing), of old time, past.
