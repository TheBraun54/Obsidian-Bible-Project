# Lexicon Entry H5596

**Lemma**: סָפַח

**Transliteration (xlit)**: çâphach

**Pronunciation**: saw-fakh'

**Description**:
or שָׂפַח; (Isaiah 3:17), a primitive root; properly, to scrape out, but in certain peculiar senses (of removal or association); abiding, gather together, cleave, smite with the scab.
