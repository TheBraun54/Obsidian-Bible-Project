# Lexicon Entry H6677

**Lemma**: צַוָּאר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tsavvâʼr

**Pronunciation**: tsav-vawr'

**Description**:
or צַוָּר; (Nehemiah 3:5), or צַוָּרֹןlemma צַוָּרן missing vowel, corrected to צַוָּרֹן; (Song of Solomon 4:9), or (feminine) צַוָּארָה; (Micah 2:3), intensively from צוּר in the sense of binding; the back of the neck (as that on which burdens are bound); neck.
