# Lexicon Entry H4720

**Lemma**: מִקְדָּשׁ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: miqdâsh

**Pronunciation**: mik-dawsh'

**Description**:
or מִקְּדָשׁ; (Exodus 15:17), from קָדַשׁ; a consecrated thing or place, especially, a palace, sanctuary (whether of Jehovah or of idols) or asylum; chapel, hallowed part, holy place, sanctuary.
