# Lexicon Entry H4383

**Lemma**: מִכְשׁוֹל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: mikshôwl

**Pronunciation**: mik-shole'

**Description**:
or מִכְשֹׁל; masculine from כָּשַׁל; a stumbling-block, literally or figuratively (obstacle, enticement (specifically an idol), scruple); caused to fall, offence, [idiom] (no-) thing offered, ruin, stumbling-block.
