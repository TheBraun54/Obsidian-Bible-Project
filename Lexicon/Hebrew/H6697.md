# Lexicon Entry H6697

**Lemma**: צוּר

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tsûwr

**Pronunciation**: tsoor

**Description**:
or צֻר; from צוּר; properly, a cliff (or sharp rock, as compressed); generally, a rock or boulder; figuratively, a refuge; also an edge (as precipitous); edge, [idiom] (mighty) God (one), rock, [idiom] sharp, stone, [idiom] strength, [idiom] strong. See also בֵּית צוּר.
