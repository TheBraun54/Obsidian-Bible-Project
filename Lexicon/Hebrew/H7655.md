# Lexicon Entry H7655

**Lemma**: שִׁבְעָה

**Transliteration (xlit)**: shibʻâh

**Pronunciation**: shib-aw'

**Description**:
(Aramaic) corresponding to שֶׁבַע; {seven (as the sacred full one); also (adverbially) seven times; by implication, a week; by extension, an indefinite number}; seven (times).
