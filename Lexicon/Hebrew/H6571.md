# Lexicon Entry H6571

**Lemma**: פָּרָשׁ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: pârâsh

**Pronunciation**: paw-rawsh'

**Description**:
from פָּרָשׁ; (compare סוּס); a steed (as stretched out to a vehicle, not single nor for mounting ); also (by implication) a driver (in a chariot), i.e. (collectively) cavalry; horseman.
