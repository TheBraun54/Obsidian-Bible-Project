# Lexicon Entry H4731

**Lemma**: מַקֵּל

**Transliteration (xlit)**: maqqêl

**Pronunciation**: mak-kale

**Description**:
or (feminine) מַקְּלָה; from an unused root meaning apparently to germinate; a shoot, i.e. stick (with leaves on, or for walking, striking, guiding, divining); rod, (hand-)staff.
