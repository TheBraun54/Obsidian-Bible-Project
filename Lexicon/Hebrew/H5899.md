# Lexicon Entry H5899

**Lemma**: עִיר הַתְּמָרִים

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ʻÎyr hat-Tᵉmârîym

**Pronunciation**: eer hat-tem-aw-reem'

**Description**:
from עִיר and the plural of תָּמָר with the article interpolated; city of the palmtrees; Ir-hat-Temarim, a place in Palestine; the city of palmtrees.
