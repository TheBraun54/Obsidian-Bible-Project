# Lexicon Entry H8565

**Lemma**: תַּן

**Transliteration (xlit)**: tan

**Pronunciation**: tan

**Description**:
from an unused root probably meaning to elongate; a monster (as preternaturally formed), i.e. a sea-serpent (or other huge marine animal); also a jackal (or other hideous land animal); dragon, whale. Compare תַּנִּין.
