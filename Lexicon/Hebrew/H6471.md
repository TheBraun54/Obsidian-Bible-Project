# Lexicon Entry H6471

**Lemma**: פַּעַם

**Transliteration (xlit)**: paʻam

**Pronunciation**: pah'-am

**Description**:
or (feminine) פַּעֲמָה; from פָּעַם; a stroke, literally or figuratively (in various applications, as follow); anvil, corner, foot(-step), going, (hundred-) fold, [idiom] now, (this) [phrase] once, order, rank, step, [phrase] thrice, (often-), second, this, two) time(-s), twice, wheel.
