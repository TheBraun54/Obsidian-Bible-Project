# Lexicon Entry G2735

**Lemma**: κατόρθωμα

**Transliteration (xlit)**: katórthōma

**Pronunciation**: kat-or'-tho-mah

**Description**:
from a compound of κατά and a derivative of ὀρθός (compare διόρθωσις); something made fully upright, i.e. (figuratively) rectification (specially, good public administration):--very worthy deed.
