# Lexicon Entry G71

**Lemma**: ἄγω

**Transliteration (xlit)**: ágō

**Pronunciation**: ag'-o

**Description**:
a primary verb; properly, to lead; by implication, to bring, drive, (reflexively) go, (specially) pass (time), or (figuratively) induce:--be, bring (forth), carry, (let) go, keep, lead away, be open.
