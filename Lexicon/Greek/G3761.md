# Lexicon Entry G3761

**Lemma**: οὐδέ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: oudé

**Pronunciation**: oo-deh'

**Description**:
from οὐ and δέ; not however, i.e. neither, nor, not even:--neither (indeed), never, no (more, nor, not), nor (yet), (also, even, then) not (even, so much as), + nothing, so much as.
