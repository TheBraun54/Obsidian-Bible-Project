# Lexicon Entry G2673

**Lemma**: καταργέω

**Transliteration (xlit)**: katargéō

**Pronunciation**: kat-arg-eh'-o

**Description**:
from κατά and ἀργέω; to be (render) entirely idle (useless), literally or figuratively:--abolish, cease, cumber, deliver, destroy, do away, become (make) of no (none, without) effect, fail, loose, bring (come) to nought, put away (down), vanish away, make void.
