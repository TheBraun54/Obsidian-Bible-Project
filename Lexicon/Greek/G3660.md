# Lexicon Entry G3660

**Lemma**: ὀμνύω

**Transliteration (xlit)**: omnýō

**Pronunciation**: om-o'-o

**Description**:
a prolonged form of a primary,                         but obsolete , for which                         another prolonged form            is used in certain tenses; to swear, i.e. take (or declare on) oath:--swear.
