# Lexicon Entry G5206

**Lemma**: υἱοθεσία

**Transliteration (xlit)**: huiothesía

**Pronunciation**: hwee-oth-es-ee'-ah

**Description**:
from a presumed compound of υἱός and a derivative of τίθημι; the placing as a son, i.e. adoption (figuratively, Christian sonship in respect to God):--adoption (of children, of sons).
