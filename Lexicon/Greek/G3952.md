# Lexicon Entry G3952

**Lemma**: παρουσία

**Transliteration (xlit)**: parousía

**Pronunciation**: par-oo-see'-ah

**Description**:
from the present participle of πάρειμι; a being near, i.e. advent (often, return; specially, of Christ to punish Jerusalem, or finally the wicked); (by implication) physically, aspect:--coming, presence.
