# Lexicon Entry G645

**Lemma**: ἀποσπάω

**Transliteration (xlit)**: apospáō

**Pronunciation**: ap-os-pah'-o

**Description**:
from ἀπό and σπάω; to drag forth, i.e. (literally) unsheathe (a sword), or relatively (with a degree of force implied) retire (personally or factiously):--(with-)draw (away), after we were gotten from.
