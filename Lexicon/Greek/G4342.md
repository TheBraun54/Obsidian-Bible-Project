# Lexicon Entry G4342

**Lemma**: προσκαρτερέω

**Transliteration (xlit)**: proskarteréō

**Pronunciation**: pros-kar-ter-eh'-o

**Description**:
from πρός and καρτερέω; to be earnest towards, i.e. (to a thing) to persevere, be constantly diligent, or (in a place) to attend assiduously all the exercises, or (to a person) to adhere closely to (as a servitor):--attend (give self) continually (upon), continue (in, instant in, with), wait on (continually).
