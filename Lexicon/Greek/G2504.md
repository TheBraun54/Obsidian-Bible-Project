# Lexicon Entry G2504

**Lemma**: κἀγώ

**Transliteration (xlit)**: kagṓ

**Pronunciation**: kam-eh'

**Description**:
from καί and ἐγώ;   so also the dative case          ,  and accusative case                and (or also, even, etc.) I, (to) me:--(and, even, even so, so) I  (also, in like wise), both me, me also.
