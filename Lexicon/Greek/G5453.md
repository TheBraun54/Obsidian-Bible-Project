# Lexicon Entry G5453

**Lemma**: φύω

**Transliteration (xlit)**: phýō

**Pronunciation**: foo'-o

**Description**:
a primary verb; probably originally, to "puff" or blow, i.e. to swell up; but only used in the implied sense, to germinate or grow (sprout, produce), literally or figuratively:--spring (up).
