# Lexicon Entry G5318

**Lemma**: φανερός

**Transliteration (xlit)**: phanerós

**Pronunciation**: fan-er-os'

**Description**:
from φαίνω; shining, i.e. apparent (literally or figuratively); neuter (as adverb) publicly, externally:--abroad, + appear, known, manifest, open (+ -ly), outward (+ -ly).
